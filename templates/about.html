About Us

This project is aimed at performing Market Basket Analysis using Association Rule Mining techniques like Apriori and FP-Growth.

Our goal is to uncover relationships between items that customers purchase together, which can help in optimizing store layouts, improving product recommendations, and enhancing customer experiences.

We utilize machine learning models to analyze purchase patterns and generate insights.
